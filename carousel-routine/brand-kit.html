Logo Guidelines

  • Minimum clear space = 1× the W mark height on all sides.
  • Never stretch, rotate, recolor, or add drop shadows to the mark.
  • Do not use the white mark on light backgrounds — use the black version.
  • On photos/busy backgrounds, use a dark pill/container behind the wordmark.

07 — Voice & Tone

Direct

No filler words. No fluff. Get to the point fast.

Action-Biased

Every post should push toward doing, not just knowing.

Honest

Real numbers, real stories. No hype without receipts.

Peer-Level

Talk like a sharp founder peer, not a brand or a guru.

Do
"Joined with zero idea. ₹3,400 by day 22. The accountability was real."
Don't
"Our world-class community empowers you to unlock your entrepreneurial potential."
Do
"₹4,999/yr. Less than ₹14/day. One good idea pays for itself."
Don't
"At an unbeatable price point, we offer incredible value for your investment."
Do
"No courses. No tutorials. Just founders shipping things."
Don't
"We provide a comprehensive learning ecosystem with curated educational content."

Social Post Color Rules

  • Always use #030712 or #0f172a as the post background — never white or grey.
  • Cyan = highlight key stat or action word. Max 1–2 per post.
  • Amber = urgency / founding member / limited availability.
  • Violet = roadmap, community, future features.
  • Emerald = wins, member results, live indicators.
  • Always include the wordmark "Founders Wing" or the W mark as a watermark in corner.
